Automated Creating of Online Schedules

Shifton online system is designed to optimize time and resources in creating employee work schedules. Shifton is an excellent solution for companies of all industries and sizes.

Teamwork optimization

Shifton will help a company save money by minimizing downtime. The system automatically assigns shifts to employees, ensuring a smooth and uninterrupted workflow at the enterprise.

Shift and Break Management

Create comfortable work schedules that take into account your employees' personal circumstances. Workers will appreciate the ability to swap shifts or breaks with colleagues, and in case of an employee replacement, Shifton will instantly adjust any schedule.
